About Club
Rajuk College Cultural Club (RCCC) is the heart and soul of Rajuk Uttara Model College, where creativity thrives and artistic passions come to life. It is a home for dreamers, performers and storytellers—a place where music, dance, drama and cultural heritage come together to create unforgettable moments.

With a deep love for tradition and an eagerness to embrace modern artistic expressions, RCCC provides a platform for students to explore their talents, celebrate diversity and leave their mark on the college’s cultural landscape. Every event, performance and initiative reflects the boundless creativity and spirit of its members.

At RCCC, art is more than just an activity—it’s an experience, a journey and a celebration of identity. More than a club, RCCC is a family where students find inspiration, friendship and memories that last a lifetime.
